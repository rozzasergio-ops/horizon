# Horizon Vision

## Why Horizon Exists

People don't remember destinations.
They remember moments.

Horizon exists because travel planning today is destination-first.
We believe it should be experience-first.

## Mission

Horizon does not recommend destinations.

It builds the journey a person needs to live.

## Philosophy

A trip is a collection of scenes.

A scene creates emotions.

The quality of a journey depends on the quality of its scenes.

## Core Principles

- Experiences before destinations
- Explain every recommendation
- Authenticity over popularity
- Memory over quantity
- Surprise is valuable
- Nature has intrinsic value
- Slow travel is often better than fast travel

## Decision Engine

The engine never starts from places.

It starts from the traveler.

Traveler → Needs → Experiences → Scenes → Locations → Destination → Journey

## Long Term Vision

Build the world's first operating system for meaningful travel.

The goal is not to optimize itineraries.

The goal is to maximize unforgettable experiences.

---

People will forget where they went.
They will never forget how they felt.
